Footer-Reveal.js

Footer-reveal.js is a jQuery plugin I wrote which enables you to quickly and easily implement a responsive sticky footer with a reveal effect on your page.

My Ambition

To make it easier for developers/designers to implement this effect on their website, particularly those that require a fluid-height footer.
